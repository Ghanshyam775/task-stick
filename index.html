Air Services

Air Services

Experience swift and efficient air freight solutions with RK Intercontinental. Our dedicated air cargo services ensure timely delivery of goods across the globe. With a network of trusted partners and a commitment to on-time performance, we guarantee your cargo reaches its destination safely and promptly.

Sea Services

Sea Services

Navigate the seas with confidence using our comprehensive sea freight services. From Full Container Loads (FCL) to Less than Container Loads (LCL), we offer flexible solutions tailored to your shipping requirements. Trust in our expertise to manage your sea shipments, ensuring cost-effectiveness and reliability.

Road Services

Road Services

Our road freight services provide seamless connectivity between destinations. With a well-maintained fleet, experienced drivers, and a strong logistics network, we offer efficient and secure overland transportation solutions for your cargo.

Warehouse Management

Warehouse Management

Experience efficient inventory management and secure storage with our state-of-the-art warehouses. Equipped with advanced tracking systems and managed by experienced professionals, our warehouses ensure your goods are stored safely and can be accessed whenever needed.

Commercial Management

Commercial Management

At RK Intercontinental, we excel in providing top-tier commercial management services that streamline your business operations and maximize profitability. Our dedicated team of experts leverages their industry insights and market knowledge to optimize your trading and freight processes.

Crew Management

Crew Management

A well-managed crew is essential for smooth sailing in the shipping industry. At RK Intercontinental, we take pride in our comprehensive crew management services. From recruitment and training to ensuring compliance with industry regulations, we ensure that your crew is not only skilled and qualified but also motivated and committed to maintaining the highest standards of safety and efficiency on board.
